# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in our community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Values

- 🤝 **Respectful**: We value differing opinions and backgrounds
- 🙏 **Inclusive**: We welcome everyone regardless of experience level
- 📚 **Supportive**: We help each other learn and grow
- 🎯 **Collaborative**: We work together toward common goals
- 🔍 **Transparent**: We communicate openly and honestly

## Expected Behavior

Be kind to others:
- Use inclusive language
- Be open to constructive criticism
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy toward others
- Give credit when credit is due

Examples of positive behavior:
- Using welcoming language
- Welcoming different viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what's best for the community
- Showing empathy

## Unacceptable Behavior

Behavior that will not be tolerated:
- Harassment, discrimination, or violence
- Exclusionary comments or jokes
- Unwelcome advances or sexual comments
- Trolling or insulting/derogatory comments
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Any form of discrimination

## Enforcement

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:
1. Report it to the maintainers
2. Include specific details and context
3. Do not engage with the offender

Maintainers will:
- Investigate reports promptly
- Maintain confidentiality
- Take appropriate action
- Communicate decisions

## Consequences

Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal from community
