The Problem
Detected too late
Chronic diseases claim millions of lives annually — mostly because they go undetected until advanced stages.

Late Detection

🔍

No Early Tools

High Treatment Cost

Prevention is 10× cheaper than treating late-stage disease.

Low Awareness

Our Solution

This system uses a Random Forest ML model to analyse your lifestyle, medical history, and family background — generating real-time risk predictions with personalised preventive guidance.

How it Works
5-step system
From input to insight in under 2 minutes.
1

Complete health questionnaire

Answer 11 questions about lifestyle habits, existing medical conditions, and family history.

2

Data cleaning & encoding

Inputs are cleaned, encoded, and preprocessed using NumPy and Pandas for accurate inference.

3

AI model inference

Trained Random Forest model generates independent risk scores for Diabetes Mellitus, CVD, and HTN.

Visualization & risk display

5

Insights, Care Compass & report
